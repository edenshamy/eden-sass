About

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale is a powerhosue vocalist and rhythm guitarist. He started singing in local bars at only age 16, gathering a group of friends to form Harvey Dale and the Cements

Amelia Brooks

Amelia Brooks is the electryifying lead guitarist of the band, raised on punk rock and electronic music. She's always growing and experimenting, with a pedal board larger than that of the average middle-aged dad-rocker.
